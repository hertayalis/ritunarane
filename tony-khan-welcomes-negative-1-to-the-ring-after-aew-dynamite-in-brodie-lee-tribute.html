Tony Khan Welcomes Negative 1 To The Ring After AEW Dynamite In Brodie Lee Tribute

Publish date: 2024-08-01

Brodie Lee passed away three years ago on December 26th. The tributes for the Exalted One flooded social media, and Seth Rollins also paid tribute to him, and Bray Wyatt in Madison Square Garden. His son was also around AEW Dynamite this week.

AEW brought a big show before Worlds Ends. That episode of Dynamite included some notable moments, including MJF losing the ROH Tag Team Titles in the main event to The Devil’s masked men.

Brodie Lee’s passing three years ago continues to be felt by pro-wrestling fans. In commemoration of his anniversary, AEW President Tony Khan brought Negative 1 to the ring after the Rampage tapings following Dynamite in Orlando.

Brodie Lee Jr has been seen on AEW television many times. It is also said that he has a contract waiting for him down the road. Only time will tell when he shows up again, and to what capacity, but it’s always good to see Negative 1.

Brodie Lee is gone, taken from us far too soon. His memory will never fade, and his son is carrying on that legacy. Hopefully, he has a long road ahead of him as he carries on the memory of his father.
