    Your Weekly Love Focus Horoscope: If you purchase an electrical item from a major store, you'll be advised to buy breakdown cover. If it goes wrong, you don't want to have to deal with the expensive costs of mending it. This week though, when it comes to an issue that's making you feel emotionally anxious, enough is enough.     July 13, 2024

    Your Weekly Horoscope: Just because humans and chickens don't, at first glance, seem to have much in common doesn't mean we don't share an important trait. We both like to establish our place in the pecking order. We're constantly assessing our position and comparing ourselves to other people to see how we shape up. With dynamic Mars linking to your ruler, Uranus, this week, stop thinking about how much you're valued and by whom. Your individuality is a gift. And you can use it to fly out of a cage-like situation, and find your freedom.

    July 12, 2024

    You're one of those folks who go out of their way to help people. Being determined (and smart), if someone you care about has a problem, you invest your time and energy trying to resolve it. It's a great attribute to possess. But you're not superhuman; your powers are limited. There's a point beyond which you shouldn't go. And, even though you're smart enough to know when you've reached it, it's not always easy for you to draw the line. This weekend, do your best. But when you can't do any more, don't do any more.

    July 11, 2024

    When a problem needs dealing with, you're a good person to have around. Your innovative way of looking at challenges means you can always think of something worth trying. Which is why you enjoy trouble-shooting. It's also why, when things are just going with the flow, you like ruffling things up! If you want to create drama today, no one will stop you. But suppose you don't? Suppose you decided to let things be and enjoy this moment of (relative) calm? The peacemaking role might not be exciting, but it's satisfying. Give it a try.

    July 10, 2024

    We might think we know what 'success' looks like, but it's harder to define than we think. Most of us think it's attained by accomplishing what we set out to achieve. But it can't be that; after all we manage to fulfil lots of different tasks, all the time; the fact that they're not particularly inspirational doesn't mean we're not being successful. You're feeling frustrated because a plan isn't going the way you want it to go. But that's not because it's going wrong. It's going better! As long as you're prepared to adapt, success is guaranteed.

    July 09, 2024

    Since all relationships are transactional, I've got a favour to ask. Would you do something for me if I do something for you? We might not be able to physically do anything for one another, but I'm hoping you get something positive from reading your prediction, just as I get something positive from writing it! But when it comes to our emotional lives, it's not easy to work out how much we're giving and how much we're receiving. Do what feels good and right to you today. And if something feels imbalanced, it's a good day to negotiate.

    July 08, 2024

    It was, apparently, US statesman Benjamin Franklin who said "the only certainties are death and taxes." When it comes to predicting what's going to happen with absolute certainty, he's got a point! Yet as someone whose job involves looking to the future, I can confidently predict that yours contains lots to get excited about. And as Venus links to your ruler today, so can you! Here comes an event that opens a portal into a more positive mindset. You can shake off a sense of pessimism, and look to the future with more hope.
