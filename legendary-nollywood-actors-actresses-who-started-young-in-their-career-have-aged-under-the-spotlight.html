Legendary Nollywood Actors &amp; Actresses Who Started Young In Their Career &amp; Have Aged Under The Spotl

Publish date: 2024-05-27

Most of the old Nollywood actors and actresses have been in the industry for over three decades. Some of these really started at a very younger age.

They rose to become what they are through consistency and in this article, we would be take a look at some of these actors and actresses.

#1 Pete Edochie

Pete Edochie began his illustrious career as a radio broadcaster in 1967 at the age of 20. He was a media person before starring in Chinua Achebe’s, THINGS FALL APART. He becomes very popular after that movie.

#2 Ngozi Ezeonu

Ngozi Ezeonu is one of the familiar faces in Nollywood. Currently, she is 55 years and she has been in the industry for over two decades with her first movie dating back to 1993.

#3 Onyeka Onwenu

The beautiful Onyeka Onwenu started her illustrious career at the age of 29. She is now 68 years and this makes her one of the few who grew in the spotlight. In addition to her acting prowess, she is also a songwriter.

#4 Kenneth Okonkwo

Kenneth Okonkwo starred in his first Nollywood movie in 1992 at the age of 24. He is currently 51 years and still an active member of Nollywood.

#5 Olu Jacobs

One of the most successful actors of his generation. He has been in the industry for about 4 decades as he starred in his first movie in the year 1970.
