French Stewart Bio And Career Begining

Before becoming a big name in the film industry, French Stewart toured in regional theatres for seven years. He had the chance in 1992 to play the role of Razor Dee on the final season of “The New WKRP in Cincinnati”. He earned his Screen Actors Guild card whiles working for Hanna-Barbera’s Shakey Quakey but was later expelled for removing the head of his costume in front of children.

Stewart was cast on the 3rd Rock from the sun which lasted for six seasons from 1996. His performance landed him in a lot of commercials and even as a spokesperson for the beverage “Clamato”. His jokes and facial expression could crack up people into laughter and he was really the master of the craft.

French Stewart had the chance to feature in some movies because of his hard work. He had his film debut in 1994 in the movie “Stargate”. Stewart also played the role of Lieutenant Louis Ferretti. He also featured in some of the movies like “Leaving Las Vegas” (1995), “The Poison Tasters” (1995), “Magic Island” (1995), “Glory Daze” (1996) and many more.

Apart from the films, Stewart has also been featured on several series which include “MADtv”, “The Opposite”, “I Love the” and others.
