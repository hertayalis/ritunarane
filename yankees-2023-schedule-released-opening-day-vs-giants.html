Yankees 2023 schedule released: Opening Day vs. Giants

Publish date: 2024-08-29

Though they have plenty to worry about at present, the Yankees’ 2023 schedule is here.

The entire 2023 MLB schedule was released on Wednesday, and with it comes the news the Yankees will open the 2023 season at home against the Giants on March 30.

With the 2023 MLB schedule marking the first time every team will play every other team in at least one series, that means fewer games against the likes of the Red Sox. Here’s the Yankees’ full schedule for 2023.
