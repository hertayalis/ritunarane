Who Has Michelle Rodriguez Dated?

Publish date: 2024-08-28

In 2014, Michelle Rodriguez, a well-known Hollywood actress, started dating her co-star Zac Efron. The pair originally met while spending their vacations together in Sardinia, Italy, where the initial spark was ignited between the two. Their relationship escalated quickly, and they were frequently seen kissing and cuddling throughout their short-lived summer romance. Unfortunately, by September of the same year, the couple announced their split after two months together.

Zac Efron is a popular American actor, singer and producer, notable for his breakout role as Troy Bolton in the High School Musical Disney series. Following this, his successful career in Hollywood, in movies such as 17 Again, The Lucky One, and Baywatch, further helped raise his profile.
