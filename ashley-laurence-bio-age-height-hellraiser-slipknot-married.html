Ashley Laurence: Scream Queen

Though there is not much to say about her early life, Ashley rose to stardom from her acting. So, how did she start? Ashley began her career from the small screen first. She made a guest appearance on the 1984 CBS soap opera titled Capitol. 

Ashley played a recurring role named Brenda Clegg on the television series. After that, she appeared in another television series titled ‘Highway to Heaven.’ Her other honorable roles in the television comprise of other shows such as Monsters(1989), Hunter(1989), Legend(1995), Hercules: The Legendary Journeys(1996), Suddenly Susan(1997), Gentle Ben(2002), Gentle Ben 2: Danger on the Mountain(2003), ER(2004), and so on.

But her acting is not limited to silver screen only. In 1987, she debuted in the British horror movie Hellraiser as Kristy Cotton. She played alongside other actors like Clare Higgins and Doug Bradley.

Not to mention, the movie was a critical and commercial success that grossed more than $14 million at the American box office. Following the success of the film, the sequel titled Hellbound: Hellraiser II was released the next year.
