Dua Lipa &amp; Anwar Hadid Make Their Red-Carpet Debut at AMAs

Publish date: 2024-05-17
  • At the 47th Annual American Music Awards last night, pop star Dua Lipa hopped on the red carpet with beau Anwar Hadid to show off some major PDA.
  • This is the first time the two walked the red carpet together, although Lipa and Hadid have been romantically linked since June.
  • During the AMAs, Lipa also performed her new single, "Don't Start Now."

Before Dua Lipa brought the house down at the 47th Annual American Music Awards with a showstopping performance of her new hit single, "Don't Start Now," she was showing off some major PDA moments with boyfriend Anwar Hadid on the red carpet.

The two shamelessly cozied up to each other while making their red-carpet debut. While Lipa stunned in Bvlgari High jewelry and a hot-pink Miu Miu gown, with a black velvet bow fastened at the waist, Hadid went casual with an Iron Maiden T-shirt and a black blazer.

The couple, who have been romantically linked since June, generously shared a big kiss for all the cameras.

Rich Fury//Getty Images

Later on in their seats inside of the Microsoft Theater, Lipa and Hadid also proudly held hands while posing for more photos.

John Shearer/AMA2019//Getty Images

This isn't the first time the two have unabashedly indulged in PDA moments. At the MTV Europe Music Awards earlier this month, Hadid was spotted adorably planting a giant kiss on Lipa's cheek during the show.

During the ceremony, Lipa gave another knockout performance of the disco single "Don't Start Now," with Hadid undoubtedly cheering her on from the audience.
