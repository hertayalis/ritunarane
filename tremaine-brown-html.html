Tremaine Brown Jr. - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-14

Actor who is widely known for his role as Boo-Boo Kipling on the Netflix original series The Get Down. He debuted on the show during its 2016 1st season.

Before Fame

He performed original rap material on the New York City subway system. He was discovered there by a casting director for The Get Down.

Trivia

He was featured in a Teen Vogue magazine article from May 2016 detailing his Met Gala look. He was subsequently profiled in a New York Post article from August 2016.

Family Life

His father helped push him to perform rap on New York City's subway.

Associated With

He read for The Get Down's creator and prolific director Baz Luhrmann for his first ever audition.
