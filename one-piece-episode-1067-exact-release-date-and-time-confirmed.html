One Piece episode 1067 exact release date and time, confirmed

Publish date: 2024-07-02

One Piece continues to chug full-steam ahead through its twentieth season, the Wano Country Arc, which commenced back in July 2019 and has yet to conclude as of the time of writing. The latest episode (the 1066th overall), “Here Comes Main Act! Powerful Techniques of Shockwave and Magnetism,” aired on Crunchyroll on June 25, which means Saturday, July 1, we’ll be getting the 1067th episode and continuing the saga of the Straw Hats vs. Big Mom.

Recommended Videos

In the aforementioned episode, which adapts Chapter 1039 of the manga, we saw Eustass Kid on the brink of death and experiencing near-death flashbacks. Meanwhile, Momonosuke determined to save lives at any cost, continues to pull Onigashima away from the Flower Capital. Big Mom fights with Kid and Law, managing to send her crashing into a nearby tower, impale her with Law’s sword and eventually unleash Puncture Wille and Damned Punk on her. Kid and Law hope to send Big Mom plummeting through the Earth’s core, but instead, she withstands the beam fired at her by Kid, who hopes to send her through the hole that Law created.

As mentioned, the upcoming episode “To the New Era! Settled! The Determination of the Brats” will air on July 1. Below are the specific times for each major timezone, including The Americas, Europe and Australia.

  • Pacific Time – 6pm on Saturday 1st July
  • Eastern Time – 9pm on Saturday 1st July
  • UK Time – 2am on Sunday 2nd July
  • European Time – 3am on Sunday 2nd July
  • India Time – 7:30am on Sunday 2nd July
  • Philippine Time – 10am on Sunday 2nd July
  • Australia Eastern Daylight Time – 1pm on Sunday 2nd July

One Piece Episode 1067 will be available to watch on Crunchyroll. Netflix also dropped the initial trailer for the live-action One Piece TV show.
