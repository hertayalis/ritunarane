Aubrey O'Day Rips Chrisean Rock &amp; Lil Mabu: &quot;We Need The Gate Keepers Back&quot;

Publish date: 2024-08-04

Aubrey O'Day called out Chrisean Rock and Lil Mabu on Twitter, following the release of their new collaboration, "MR. TAKE YA B*TCH." O'Day remarked that she's embarrassed for the new generation of artists. The release of the song comes amid rumors of a romance between Rock and Mabu.

"I’m so embarrassed for this new generation," O'Day wrote in his post. "We did not walk so this trash music could run. We need the gate keepers back.. never thought I’d say it, but everything shouldn’t b allowed to enter the sacred space of real artists who practice their craft with discipline & respect."

Fans of O'Day's mostly agreed with the opinion. One user replied: "The industry did itself such a disservice when it allowed streaming and TikTok to affect the charts. Anyone with a social media following can be an artist now. It’s sad because real talent is being overlooked for trends. Smh." Another wrote: "Thank you @AubreyODay! I have been saying the same thing about this garbage being put out as music when so many artists had to work hard and know how to put on a good show!" Check out O'Day's full post below.
